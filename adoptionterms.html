Fur-Ever Friends Foundation - Pet Adoption Terms and Conditions

1. No Refund Policy

All adoption bookings are final. We do not offer refunds for adoptions once they are purchased. Please ensure you have selected the appropriate pet before completing your payment.

2. No-Show and Cancellation Policy

If you fail to show up for your scheduled adoption appointment within 15 minutes without providing prior notice, your appointment will be considered canceled. In such instances, you will need to re-purchase an adoption appointment should you wish to reschedule. You can re-book via this link: rewrite your adoption form to reschedule an adoption appointment

Kindly email us or message us on one of our social media pages to get the schedule link where you will choose your time slot.

We understand that unforeseen circumstances can arise. If you cannot attend your scheduled appointment, please inform us at least 10 hours in advance to avoid cancellation.

3. Golden Tails Subscription Plan

If your adoption is part of the Golden Tails Subscription Plan, special conditions apply:

You are allowed a grace period of 15 minutes from the start time of your appointment. If you do not join the appointment within this time frame, your appointment will be considered canceled.

Post the 15-minute grace period, to reschedule your appointment, you will be required to purchase a new session. You can re-book it via this link: Book an Adoption Appointment

Kindly email us or text our adoption coordinator to get the schedule link where you will choose your time slot.

4. Adoption Fees

The adoption fee is 250. This fee helps cover the costs of medical care, vaccinations, food, and shelter for the pets while they are in our care. By paying this fee, you are not only adopting a pet but also supporting our mission to rescue and care for more animals in need.

We understand that this fee may seem high, but it is essential to ensure that each pet receives the best possible care before finding their forever home. Your contribution helps us provide a safe and loving environment for all our animals, giving them a second chance at life.

5. Health and Safety

All pets available for adoption have undergone a health check and are up-to-date with vaccinations. However, we recommend that you take your new pet to a veterinarian for a full check-up within the first week of adoption.

6. Adoption Requirements

Adopters must be at least 18 years old and provide valid identification. A home visit may be required to ensure a suitable environment for the pet.

7. Post-Adoption Support

We offer post-adoption support to help you and your new pet adjust. Please contact us if you have any questions or need assistance.

8. Return Policy

If you are unable to keep the pet, please contact us. We will work with you to find a suitable solution, which may include returning the pet to our care.

9. Privacy Policy

Your personal information will be kept confidential and used only for the purposes of the adoption process. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent.
